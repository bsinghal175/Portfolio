Bharat Singhal

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical & Systems Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis. As a graduate research assistant at the Applied Mathematics lab , I am interested primarily in the area of control theory, optimization, network science, and nonlinear dynamics under the supervision of Jr-Shin Li. My research focuses on applying tools from control theory and optimization to understand complex networks of nonlinear units and effectively regulate their collective behavior
